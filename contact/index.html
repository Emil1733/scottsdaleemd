Phase 1: Technical Intake (0-4 Hours)

Once your form is submitted, our project coordinators conduct an immediate **Subterranean Data Audit** of your property. We consult local Maricopa County geological maps and our internal archive of over 1,500 local projects to identify potential caliche zones, granite shelf interference, or specific neighborhood utility density. We don't just "read your email"; we analyze your land.

🔍

Phase 2: Geotechnical Site Walk (24-48 Hours)

If your project meets our pre-qualification standards, we schedule a mandatory site walk-through. This isn't a "sales visit." It is a technical inspection where we measure gate clearance (checking for the 7-foot excavator standard), identify "Private Utilities" (pool gas and electrical), and assess the structural integrity of your home’s foundation in relation to the pool shell. This ensures your estimate is a flat-rate, binding quote—not an "allowance."

📝

Phase 3: The Binding Engineering Quote (72 Hours)

Within 72 hours of our site visit, you will receive a comprehensive **Project Blueprint and Binding Quote**. This document details our exact plan for shell extraction, backfill material selection (native vs. imported ABC), and a guaranteed timeline for MCESD and Rule 310 permit sign-off. This level of technical transparency is why Arroyo is the preferred partner for Scottsdale's most prestigious estate managers and real estate professionals.
