THE "SINKING" PROBLEM

Why Your Yard Sinks
(And How We Stop It)

When dirt is simply dumped into a pool cavity, it contains millions of micro-voids (air pockets). Over time, gravity and desert monsoons cause the soil to collapse into these pockets. This is called "settlement," and it ruins landscaping investment.

🛠️ **The 12-Inch Lift Method**: We never fill the whole pool at once. We fill in specific 12-inch layers called "lifts," compacting each one with mechanical vibrating plates before moving to the next.
💧 **Moisture Conditioning**: Dry Arizona soil won't compact. We optimize the moisture level of every lift using high-precision water application to achieve maximum Proctor density.
📈 **95% Proctor Standard**: This is the benchmark required by Maricopa City Engineers. It ensures the ground is as stable as—or more stable than—undisturbed native soil.

ENGINEERING PROCESS

Anatomy of a Build-Ready Fill

We don't just fill a hole; we engineer a foundation. Here is the layer-by-layer reality.

Layer 1: Drainage

Remaining shell is perforated with 18" drainage ports to prevent "pool-tub" water accumulation from hydrostatic pressure.

Layer 2-5: Managed Lifts

Alternating 12" soil lifts, moisture-treated and mechanically vibrated to eliminate all subterranean air pockets.

Layer 6: Proctor Test

Geotechnical density verification at the 24" mark to ensure the 95% threshold has been exceeded for structural load.

Layer 7: Scape-Ready

Final 6" topsoil layer, graded to match the existing yard pitch for perfect surface runoff management.
