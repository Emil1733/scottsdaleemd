ENGINEERING OVERVIEW

What Partial Demolition Actually Means

🏗️

Shell Breaking

The top 18–36 inches of the concrete shell are systematically broken using hydraulic impact hammers. The broken concrete is left in place to form a drainage layer.

💧

Drainage Engineering

Multiple drainage holes are punched through the remaining shell, preventing future water accumulation. Critical in Scottsdale's seasonal monsoon environment.

📦

Mechanical Backfill

The void above the broken shell is filled with clean, certified dirt in compacted layers. We use vibratory plate compactors on every 12-inch lift.

📐

Final Grading

The site is laser-graded to match your existing landscape pitch. Your landscaper can begin work within 48 hours of project completion.
