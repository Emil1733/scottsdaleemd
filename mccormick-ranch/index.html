Will my HOA allow pool removal?

Yes, most Scottsdale HOAs approve removals as long as you follow professional drainage protocols and Rule 310 dust control. We provide the full documentation package for you.

How long does the project take?

Typical McCormick Ranch removals take 4–6 business days from first break to final grading. This includes the Maricopa permit inspection window.

Can I build a patio over the area?

If you choose our full extraction and certified 95% backfill, yes. The surface becomes structurally sound for any future construction, including guest houses.

Is there caliche in McCormick Ranch?

Generally, McCormick Ranch has softer soil than North Scottsdale, but we often encounter "hardpan" layers. We use hydraulic breakers to ensure proper vertical drainage regardless of soil type.
