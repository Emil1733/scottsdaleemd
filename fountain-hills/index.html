FAQ

Fountain Hills Pool Removal Questions

Will the rock breaking vibrate my house?

Many Fountain Hills homes are built on rock slabs. We use specialized "Impact Damping" pre-cuts to isolate the pool shell from your home's foundation before heavy rock-breaking begins.

Do you work with the Firerock HOA?

Yes. We are familiar with the architectural guidelines of Firerock, Sunridge Canyon, and other major communities. We provide the drawings and project plans they require for approval.

How is drainage handled on hillside lots?

We ensure the "Pool Tub" effect is impossible by punching deep vertical drainage ports through the shell and laser-grading the surface to direct water into existing yard washes.

Can I build a new deck over the pool area?

Yes—if you choose our Engineered Structural Fill. We certify the ground to 95% compaction, making it eligible for pavers, decks, and outdoor kitchen structures.
