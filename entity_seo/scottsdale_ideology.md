1. The Homepage: The "Market Authority" Hub
   The homepage is typically the most powerful page on your site. Its goal is to rank for your most competitive and profitable "money" keywords.

Target: High-intent, broad search terms (e.g., "Pool Removal Scottsdale" or "Pool Demolition Arizona").

Role: It acts as the central hub that receives the "cumulative authority" from all the smaller, more specific sub-pages. It must reference "Caliche," "Rule 310," and "Paradise Valley" to anchor the domain.

2. Service Pages: The "Expertise" Builders
   The goal of these pages is to prove to Google that you are a subject matter expert in the specific geotechnical challenges of Scottsdale.

Target: Specific entities/sub-topics (e.g., "Caliche Rock Excavation," "95% Certified Backfill," or "Dust Control Compliance").

Role: They provide a "Density of Authority." By having a page for every common problem (like the 'hard dig' in North Scottsdale), you demonstrate deep domain knowledge.

Instruction: Do not keyword stuff. Instead, explain the chemistry of caliche or the physics of vibratory compaction. Make them genuinely helpful to humans.

3. Location Pages: The "Context" Anchors
   For Scottsdale, these pages show Google the specific high-value neighborhoods you serve.

Target: Specific luxury enclaves (e.g., "Paradise Valley," "Silverleaf," "DC Ranch," or "Troon North").

Role: They signal to Google's knowledge graph that your business is physically and contextually relevant to the specific high-value areas you want to rank in.

Instruction: Talk about the specific service problems unique to that location (e.g., why Silverleaf lots have specific mountain-granite excavation needs vs. the flat lots of McCormick Ranch).

4. "Zero Search Volume" Pages: The "Authority Density" Secret
   Some pages are designed to target technical terms that tools say have zero search volume.

Goal: To show Google's algorithm that you haven't skipped any regulatory or technical details.

Role: Pages like "Maricopa County Rule 310 Dust Control" or "Proctor Density Testing" help the entire domain rank better by increasing your "Authority Density" score.

Summary of the "Network" Goal:
The ultimate goal of all these pages together is to lower your relationship score in Google’s systems. By linking all these highly related entities (Geology + Local Law + Neighborhoods) back to your homepage through the "Global Navigation," you prove you are the most relevant result for the Scottsdale market.
