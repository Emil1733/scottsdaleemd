### 2. How Google Measures Our Authority
Google uses two primary signals to measure our entity relationship to the Scottsdale market:

1.  **Sitemap Depth:** The total list of specific "sub-entities" we cover (e.g., Caliche Excavation vs. Granite Digging, Full Extraction vs. MCESD Abandonment).
2.  **Entity Proximity:** How closely related our pages are.

    *   **High Score (Bad):** "Pool Removal" + "General Landscaping" + "Kitchen Remodeling." (This dilutes our expert status).
