FAQ

Cave Creek & Carefree Questions

Is a pool removal more expensive in Cave Creek?

Typically, yes—the abundance of granite bedrock usually requires more machine-hours with hydraulic breakers. Most projects add $1,500 - $3,000 for rock-specific labor.

Do you handle the permit with the Town of Cave Creek?

Yes. We manage both the Town of Cave Creek/Carefree building permits and the Maricopa County Environmental health filings (MCESD).

Can I build a guest house over the pool?

If you choose our full extraction and 95% certified backfill, yes. This makes the surface structurally sound for casitas, ADUs, or workshops.

How is dust controlled in the desert breeze?

We use active misting systems that saturate the shell as we break, ensuring Rule 310 compliance even during the afternoon desert breeze.
